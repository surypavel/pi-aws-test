🧁 Delicious Cupcake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar, flour, butter, eggs, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add in the milk and vanilla extract and mix until smooth.
  4. Scoop the batter into cupcake liners.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before frosting.
